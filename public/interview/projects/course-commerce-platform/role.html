03My Role & Scope

What I owned, and what I didn't

I owned

  • Frontend architecture for the multi-environment course sales & payment flow (WeChat, Alipay, H5).
  • Shared component library — UI primitives, course cards, order and payment components used across all three runtimes.
  • Project scaffolding and engineering standards adopted by the mobile commerce team.
  • Performance baseline and monitoring on the core transaction funnel.

I partnered on / didn't own

  • Payment backend and order service — owned by the trade backend team; I defined the client contracts.
  • Business requirements and promotion logic — driven by product; I implemented the client-side rules.
  • Native / SDK integration for WeChat & Alipay payment — partnered with the platform team.

Team: worked inside a cross-functional squad of ~6 engineers (frontend, backend, QA). My lane was the frontend surface; the wider transaction stack was a shared effort.
