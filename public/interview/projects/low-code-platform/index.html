01Overview

What it is, in one sentence

A low-code platform where non-engineers compose marketing campaign pages by dragging and configuring schema-driven components, preview them in real time, and publish to production without engineering involvement.

Situation
Campaign pages were built one-by-one by frontend engineers, creating a bottleneck and wasting engineering time on repeated layout work.
Task
Remove engineering from the campaign delivery path while keeping visual and brand quality consistent.
Action
Designed a schema-driven rendering engine, a shared component library, and a drag-and-drop editor; drove adoption across 4 business lines.
Result
Campaign launch time dropped from days to minutes; frontend team shifted from delivery to platform work.
