03My Role & Scope

What I owned, and what I didn't

I owned

  • End-to-end technical architecture of the platform (editor, runtime, build pipeline).
  • Schema design and the rendering engine contract.
  • Shared component library APIs and the extension model.
  • Adoption strategy — working directly with product & ops stakeholders across business lines.
  • Engineering conventions, project scaffolding, and internal docs.

I partnered on / didn't own

  • Backend services for schema storage, versioning, and publish workflow — owned by a backend engineer.
  • Visual / brand language — owned by design; I implemented the component spec.
  • Experiment & analytics integration — I defined the hooks, platform team integrated.

Team: myself as tech lead plus 3–5 engineers depending on phase. I was the only person who spanned editor, runtime, and adoption — which mattered because most design choices only made sense when you saw all three surfaces at once.
